The Georgia Milestones Assessment System is a comprehensive assessment program spanning grades 3-12. It measures how well students have learned the knowledge and skills outlined in the state-adopted content standards.

About NWEA MAP Testing

NWEA MAP (Measures of Academic Progress) is a computerized adaptive test that adjusts difficulty based on student responses. It provides precise measurement of student achievement and growth over time.

✓ Adaptive difficulty
✓ Personalized testing
✓ RIT score reporting
✓ Growth tracking

GADOE Standards

Aligned with Georgia Department of Education K-12 Standards

📚 Mathematics Standards

Our math curriculum covers Number & Operations, Algebra, Geometry, Measurement, and Data Analysis aligned with Georgia Standards of Excellence (GSE).

📝 English Language Arts Standards

ELA content includes Reading Literary & Informational Texts, Writing, Speaking & Listening, and Language standards.

🔬 Science Standards

Science curriculum follows Georgia Performance Standards covering Physical Science, Earth Science, Life Science, and Scientific Inquiry.

🌎 Social Studies Standards

Social Studies content aligns with Georgia standards for History, Geography, Government/Civics, and Economics.
